Affordable Solutions

Our no frills business model is such that we're able to offer  professional grade cctv equipment at significantly lower installed costs than our larger competitors.  

 

Our company is nimble, responsive, and constantly evolving and growing.  Our modus operandi is deliberately sensitive to nuances of the local market.  Prices are therefore important.  Value is also important, with the latest and internationally acceptable brands and standards being made available to our clients.

Wide range of installations

Our partnerships with a wide range of professionals enables JESL to provide a signifant range of offerings ...from domestic cctv installations to community /  public space monitoring and surveillance.

JESL primarily installs surveillance equipment from Global leaders in surveillance cameras and cctv equipment manufacture - such as Hikvision and Axis.

 

Other smaller but reliable brands are carried when expressly required by our clients.  

As such, solutions are tailored always to accomodate the needs of our clients.
